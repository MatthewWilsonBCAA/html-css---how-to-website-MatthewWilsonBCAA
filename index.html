How to Skyrim

Section 1: Early Game

Step 1: Boot the Game

This step is pretty simple. Just put the disc in (if you have a physical copy), click on it, and let it load to the main menu.

Step 2: Start the Game

This step is also pretty easy. Just navigate to the option "New Game" and click it.

Step 3: Learn the Controls

The controls to this game can get tricky, so be sure to use Helgen as a training spot to learn them!

Section 2: Most of the Game

Step 1: Go to Riverwood

This step is marked. Just go to the little river-side town that the arrow points you to. Don't forget to save often!

Step 2: Do the Main Questline

Easiest section of our How To. Just follow the arrow and kill enemies that get in your way.

Step 3: Kill Alduin

By this point, you are an expert, so you should breeze through this fight. Just kill the big black dragon with the arrow over it.

Section 3: Modding (Part 1)

Step 1: Go to the Mod menu

Now that you have beat the game, you are obligated to start downloading mods. First, go back to the main menu, and click on the option that says "Mods".

Step 2: Pick a cool looking Mod

There are thousands of options to choose from. Go ahead, find one you like, but I recommend the Beyond Skyrim Mod! Then, when it is finished downloading, install it (varies from Console to PC).

Step 3: Play Mod

Now that you have downloaded and installed the mod, go ahead and boot up your save and give it a whirl!

Section 4: Modding (Part 2)

Step 1: Go back to the Mod menu

Now that you have played around with some mods, you are obligated to start downloading as many mods as your device can handle! Go get at least 40.

Step 2: Play the Mods

Once you feel like you have a good combination of mods, go ahead and boot your save.

Step 3: Watch your game crash

If you have too many mods installed, or the wrong ones, your game is probably going to crash. But, sometimes the bugs that originate are entertaining.

You are now a Skyrim expert! Thank you for following this tutorial. (Note the amount of satire shoved into this site)
